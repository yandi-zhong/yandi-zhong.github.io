鸡蛋

EGGS

Synopsis

Ever since Yandi was a little girl, her grandmother would send the eggs from her chickens to her in big paper boxes. She lost these eggs as she passed away and as she moves to bigger and bigger cities, along with something else that hides in the egg. She sets on a journey to find her grandma again.

Film Stills

“EGGS is a film about family love, the kind of love that sits in the corner and wait for you to come home. It may not be the best at expressing itself, but it will always be the sanctuary we seek. It is also about regret, the kind of regret that make you ask, “why do we not miss the water until the well runs dry?” My grandmother was a tiny little lady who was always full of energy and dedicated her whole life to her family. When I stayover at her place, I was always woken up at 5 am at the crow of her roasters every day. As I grew up and move further and further away from home, I have never heard crows that are so annoying yet so close to my heart. This film is for my grandma and for all of you who has experienced lost, who has been drifting from one place to another, and who has been trying to grasp onto something that feels home.”
--- Director's notes
