My clay craft-making journey began in 2021 during the pandemic. I sought inner strength by creating something new that reflected the beautiful aspects of a world filled with uncertainties at the time. My clay work progressively transforms from charming, whimsical creations into intricately detailed miniatures that capture the essence of everyday blessings and special memories. I hope you can find the small joys in life within them, just as I did.
