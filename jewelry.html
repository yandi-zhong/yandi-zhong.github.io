Jewelry

饰

I started to make jewelleries in the year of 2021. Based in Canada for the first two years, I have now relocated to London to complete a master's degree while continue making crafts. Using mostly polymer and sculpting clay, I designed and made earrings, necklaces and rings. I have been largely self-taught, using tutorials and practice to enhance my skills and knowledge in the jewellery-making. Most of my work are born from a simple vision or a momentary feeling I have in a sudden. I hope that every piece of my work would share a moment I wish to preserve with those wearing them, to take them back to the first purple blossom in an early spring morning, the music played by the band on a patio in a summer sunset, the yellow sunflowers standing in the autumn wind, or the snowman built with friends in your snowy backyard.

I incorporate an abundance of natural elements in my design, so a lot of my work is evolved around themes like weather, plants and animals. The pandemic pushes us back to a digital world where we held on to human warmth and connection through phones and computers. Making these jewelleries started as an attempt for me to connect with the world in another way. I describe them as my “secret garden”, my get away. I wish that you would find the small joys in life in them as I did.
