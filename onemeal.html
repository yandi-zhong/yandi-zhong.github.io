One Meal

Synopsis

When making a family meal called "crawfish etouffee", a classic Louisiana dish, the mother and daughter take us down the memory lane, in which they turn back to their childhood self and adventured into a fantasy world of family, food and love.

Film Stills

"ONE MEAL is a film that wishes to answer the question “Is recording an ordinary family's history interesting?” It may be true that your mother or your grandma is not as accomplished as big names like Bill Gates, but they are still very important in their own world. Who we become is constantly shaped by the people around us, and food is one of the simplest yest most profound love language. Every family has one meal that is just so special to them. To the Lampert family, a simple family meal like crawfish etouffee could bring their loved ones back to them and the now mothers and grandmothers back to their childhood selves. To see them making crawfish etouffee together in the present shows how a family can change as time flew by. As grandma would say, nothing compares to the joy and warmth from sharing a good home-cooked meal with family on an ordinary Saturday night."
--- Director's notes
